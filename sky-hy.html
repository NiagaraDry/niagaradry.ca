Bottles

Sky-Hy had several bottle designs over its life.

Bottle Caps

Sky-Hy went through numerous designs for their bottle caps. Designs include:

  • Standard: A standard design with "Sky-Hy Beverages Registered" and an airplane.
  • Variant 1: Flavor-specific designs in blue/white, with white text for the flavor names and "Canada's Finest" written on the lower half of the cap.
  • Variant 2: Flavor-specific designs in blue/white, with colored text for the flavor names and "Canada's Finest" written on the lower half of the cap.
  • Variant 3: Flavor-specific designs in blue/white, with colored text for the flavor names and "Canada's Finest" written on the lower half of the cap inside a detailed map of Canada.
  • Variant 4: Flavor-specific designs in blue/white, with white text for the flavor name(s) and "Canada's Finest" written on the lower half of the cap in white text with a dark background.
  • Variant 5: A (possibly flavor-specific) design in silver/red with the flavor name in the red half. I've only seen this cap in Cream Soda.

Labels

Much like the bottle caps, the labels also had several variants:

  • Variant 1: Features the flavor, "Canada's Finest" text in blue, and "Niagara Dry Beverages" in yellow along the bottom.
  • Variant 2: Features the flavor, "Canada's Finest" text in blue, and "Niagara Dry Beverages - Niagara Falls, Ont." in yellow along the bottom.
  • Variant 3: Features the flavor, "Canada's Finest" text in yellow over top of a colorful map of Canada, and "Niagara Dry Beverages - Niagara Falls, Ont." in yellow along the bottom.
